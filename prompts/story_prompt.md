# Story Generation Prompt

## Objective
Generate a short psychological sci-fi narrative exploring identity, consciousness,
and neurological integration between two individuals.

## Prompt Used

Title: "Half of Me"

Write a dark, psychological science-fiction story involving two children who
undergo an experimental neurosurgical procedure. The tone should be unsettling,
introspective, and emotionally heavy.

Core themes:
- Split consciousness
- Identity dissolution
- Neuroplasticity
- Memory bleed-through
- Ethical boundaries of neuroscience

Narrative constraints:
- Two protagonists (Elias and Jonah)
- Experimental hemisphere exchange surgery
- Gradual mental synchronization
- Shared dreams and memories
- Final convergence into a single identity (Janus)

Style guidance:
- Cinematic
- Minimal exposition
- Show psychological effects through behavior and dreams
- Avoid excessive technical jargon

Output format:
- Multi-chapter story
- Escalating tension
- Ambiguous but haunting resolution
