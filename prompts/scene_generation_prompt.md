# Scene & Video Generation Prompt

## Objective
Generate short cinematic video clips matching a dark psychological sci-fi tone.

## Prompt Used

Generate a cinematic scene with the following characteristics:
- Dark, moody lighting
- Minimal color palette (black, blue, white)
- Slow camera motion
- Psychological horror atmosphere
- No explicit gore

Scene references:
- Hospital corridors
- Surgical lighting
- Mirrors with delayed reflections
- Black lake under moonlight
- Spiral staircase descending into darkness

Style:
- Film grain
- Subtle motion
- Dream-like transitions
- Ambiguous visuals

Constraints:
- Scenes must be abstract and symbolic
- Avoid explicit facial realism
- Emphasize mood over realism
